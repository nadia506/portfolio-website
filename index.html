About Me

Hello! I'm Bokyung Nadia Kim, a dedicated software engineer passionate about creating innovative and user-friendly web applications. With a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development, I enjoy tackling challenging projects and continuously improving my skill set.

These experiences have provided me with practical knowledge and have further enhanced my technical expertise. Looking ahead, I am excited to embrace new technologies and tackle new challenges to push the boundaries of innovation.
